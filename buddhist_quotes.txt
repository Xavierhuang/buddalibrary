Daily Practice Suggestions
--------------------------

**Morning Reflection**
- Choose one quote to contemplate during your morning meditation
- Write it down and keep it with you throughout the day
- Consider how it applies to your current life situation

**Evening Review**
- Reflect on how you applied the wisdom during the day
- Notice any insights or challenges that arose
- Choose a new quote for the next day

**Weekly Practice**
- Select a theme for the week (e.g., compassion, mindfulness, impermanence)
- Read related quotes each day
- Journal about your experiences and insights

**Monthly Deepening**
- Choose a longer teaching or sutra to study
- Attend a meditation group or discussion
- Share insights with others

How to Use These Quotes
-----------------------

1. **Contemplation** - Sit quietly with a single quote for 5-10 minutes
2. **Journaling** - Write about what the quote means to you personally
3. **Discussion** - Share quotes with friends or family and discuss them
4. **Memorization** - Commit meaningful quotes to memory for daily recall
5. **Application** - Look for opportunities to apply the wisdom in daily life

Remember: "The teaching is like a raft for crossing the river. Once you have crossed, you don't carry the raft on your head."
